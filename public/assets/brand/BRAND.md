## Illustration Assets (Scott Pearson)

### `cow-character.png`
The mascot — a friendly dairy cow in classic NZ farm style, with signature bell and black-and-white markings. White background.
- **Usage:** Footer scene, hero sections, StatCallout, print, merchandise
- **Alt text:** `""` (decorative) or `"NZ Cowshed Groovers mascot — a confident dairy cow"`
- **Blend mode tip:** `mix-blend-mode: multiply` over light backgrounds makes white areas transparent

### `illustrated-landscape.png`
A wide-format illustration of crosshatch-grooved concrete stretching to the horizon, flanked by NZ green hills under a teal sky. The landscape conveys the product and environment simultaneously.
- **Usage:** Footer scene banner, section backgrounds, print
- **Alt text:** `""` (decorative) or `"Illustrated crosshatch-grooved concrete yard with NZ farmland in the background"`
- **Aspect ratio:** ~16:5 (very wide)

### `slipping-cows-cost.jpg`
Social-first marketing illustration: a dairy cow laden with money bags stands on grooved concrete at sunset. Headline: "Do You Know What Slipping Cows Cost You?" CTA: "Get the Guide."
- **Usage:** Homepage callout section, social media, campaign landing pages, print ads
- **Alt text:** `"Do you know what slipping cows cost you? — illustrated dairy cow carrying money bags on grooved concrete"`
- **Aspect ratio:** 1:1 (square)
- **Note:** Link to `/contact` when used as interactive element on web

### `grooved-concrete-cartoon.png`
A perspective-view illustration of crosshatch-grooved concrete receding to the horizon. White background.
- **Usage:** Section texture/watermark, print editorial, explainer graphics
- **Alt text:** `""` (decorative) or `"Crosshatch-grooved concrete surface illustration showing the groove pattern in perspective"`
- **Blend mode tip:** At low opacity as a background/watermark element

### `ribbon-banner.png`
The brand wordmark as a classic scroll/ribbon in deep purple, lettered "NZ COWSHED GROOVERS" in white.
- **Usage:** Footer scene overlay, printed materials, merchandise, signage
- **Alt text:** `"NZ Cowshed Groovers"` (when primary logo) or `""` (when decorative alongside other brand text)

### `scott-badge.png`
A rounded-rectangle phone badge in deep purple: "📞 Scott: 0274 747 775".
- **Usage:** Print ads, flyers, vehicle signage, social media bios
- **Alt text:** `"Call Scott: 0274 747 775"`
- **Note:** For web use prefer an accessible `<a href="tel:...">` link; use badge for print or as visual accent only
